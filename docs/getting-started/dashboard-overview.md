# Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is the home page of InsightLens. When your database is empty you'll see a **Welcome to InsightLens** card prompting you to import your first survey. Once you have data, the dashboard fills with four sections.

## Stats row

Four cards across the top summarise your database:

- **Total Units** — the number of distinct units with at least one survey.
- **Total Surveys** — the total number of survey offerings imported.
- **Avg Response Rate** — average across every imported survey.
- **Total Comments** — count of student comments extracted from those surveys.

## Trend, recent surveys, AI preview

The middle row has three cards:

- **Overall Experience Trend** — a line chart of average overall experience across semesters. Hover for exact values.
- **Recent Surveys** — the five most recently imported offerings. Click any row to jump to that unit's detail page.
- **Ask InsightLens** — a preview of the AI chat. If you haven't set up a provider, it points you at Settings. If you have, you can start a conversation straight from here.

## Top performers & units needing attention

The bottom row gives you a quick snapshot of extremes:

- **Top Performers** — units with overall experience ≥ 85 % over the last 12 months, grouped by semester. Each entry links to the unit detail page. Use the **CSV** and **Report** buttons to export the list.
- **Units Needing Attention** — units whose scores are trending down or sitting below benchmark, with the same CSV / Markdown export buttons.

These are the same lists you can open in full on the [Performance Reports](../reports/performance-reports.md) page, just cut down to what fits on the dashboard.

## Refreshing

The dashboard pulls live from your local database — there's nothing to refresh. Every time you import new surveys, the stats and cards will update the next time you visit the page.
