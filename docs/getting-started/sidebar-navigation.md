# Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar is the main way to move around InsightLens. You can collapse it to a narrow icon bar with the chevron button at the top right of the sidebar header.

On macOS, the top of the sidebar leaves room for the traffic-light window controls and is draggable — you can grab it to move the window.

## Quick Insights

Two shortcuts at the top of the sidebar jump straight to filtered views of the dashboard:

- **Trending Up** — units whose experience scores have improved semester-over-semester.
- **Need Attention** — units whose scores have slipped.

## Main navigation

- **Dashboard** — high-level summary of your database. See [Dashboard Overview](dashboard-overview.md).
- **Units** — searchable, filterable list of every unit in the database. See [Browsing & Filtering Units](../essential-workflow/unit-filtering.md).
- **Performance Reports** — full top-performers and needs-attention lists with exports. See [Performance Reports](../reports/performance-reports.md).
- **Promotion Suggestions** — units whose track record supports a promotion case. See [Promotion Suggestions](../reports/promotion-suggestions.md).
- **Ask InsightLens** — natural-language chat over your survey data. See [Asking Good Questions](../ai-chat/asking-questions.md). A small dot next to this item shows your AI connection status (green = connected, red = not connected, amber = checking).
- **Import** — drag-and-drop PDF import. See [Importing Survey PDFs](../essential-workflow/importing-data.md).
- **Documentation** — what you're reading right now.
- **Settings** — database location, AI provider, general preferences.

## App info

At the very bottom of the sidebar, an **InsightLens vX.Y.Z** button links to the About page. The version number is read directly from the running app, so it always matches the installed release.
